Past / Archived Projects

These projects are no longer being actively developed, and are available as a reference for future projects. Support is not guaranteed for these projects.

  • Eight Dollars
    A browser extension for Twitter that helps you differentiate between users that paid for verification, and users that are actually verified. Featured on Engadget and The Verge
  • Foodie (Working Title)
    A food rescue platform that makes it easier for people to volunteer with food rescue programs that feed the needy. Built in collaboration with Perfectly Imperfect.
  • A Terrible Platform (Working Title)
    A hackathon management platform. Built for the New Zealand Terrible Ideas hackathon
  • Vaxx.nz (2021)
    A website that helped 120,000+ people find earlier COVID vaccination bookings.
  • Vaxx Locations (2021)
    Raw scraped venue data from bookmyvaccine.nz - you can use it to build your own COVID vaccine finder
  • Vaxxed.as (2021)
    A website to scan and verify COVID-19 Vaccine Certificates originally built by @rafcontreras.
  • Vaxx Widget (2021)
    A website widget that helps you find available vaccine bookings
  • Lenny (2021)
    A proof of concept website that uses bank transactions to do COVID contact tracing. Built for the 2021 GovHack Hackathon
  • GLN Hashes (2021)
    Experiments around reverse engineering New Zealand COVID contact tracing QR codes - made for the 2021 GovHack Hackathon
