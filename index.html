Haxx.nz

We build real things.

Haxx.nz is a collective of software developers, designers and makers of all kinds who build things for fun. The projects we taken on or maintain range from silly proof of concept ideas, to free open source tools that do social good in New Zealand.

If you’ve got skills and want to collaboratively build something, or want to meet like minded people to work on stuff with then jump in! We also accept requests to maintain projects that you no longer want to support, but might still have real users.
